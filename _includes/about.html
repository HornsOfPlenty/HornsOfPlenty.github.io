Who are Horns?

Entertaining audiences with our lively blend of global street music, we're a band with plenty of volume! With around 35 enthusiastic members, we boast a multitude of brass, saxes, clarinets and percussion.


You'll find us anywhere the audience is smiling - at festivals, parades, carnivals and street parties across Oxfordshire and beyond.
