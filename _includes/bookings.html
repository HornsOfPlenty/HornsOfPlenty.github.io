Want to book us?

We'd love to be part of your next event. Just ping us an email at gigs@hornsofplenty.co.uk and let's make some musical magic happen!


As bands go, we're pretty low maintenance, with no need for a stage or power outlets. And while we call Oxford home, we're usually up for a road trip to bring our lively brand of world music to you.

Our rates? We're open to negotiation. Every penny goes back into our community band coffers to keep Horns of Plenty afloat.


Now, here's the small print: because Horns are a large group, it might take a little bit of coordination to lock in your date. So, the sooner you get in touch (think, at least a month beforehand), the better chance we have of making it happen!
